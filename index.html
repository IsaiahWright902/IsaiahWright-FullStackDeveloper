Projects I've Worked On:

Water Damage Estimator

In the past it took field technicians upwards of 30 minutes to create a estimate off of a scope sheet. With the help of the Water Damage Estimator, they can now create a much more accurate estimate in an average time of 5 minutes. All the technician needs to do is put in the measurements of the room they are working in and answer a few questions. This application will then automatically calculate how much equipment they will need and how much it will cost displaying it in a format that can be shown to a customer.

I worked solo on this project responsible for the front-end and back-end development of the entire application. Watching it grow to handle more and more has been nothing less than fantastic. I am proud to say I have created something that has met and surpassed the goals originally planned for it.

I have done multiple presentations on this application all over Florida and trained multiple technicians how to use it. I cant wait to see what other challenges I will face in the future.
