Projects Ive Worked On:

Water Damage Estimator

In the past it took feild technicians upwards of 30 minutes to create a estimate off of a scope sheet. With the help of the Water Damage Estimator, they can now create a much more accurate estimate in an average time of 5 minutes. All the technician needs to do is put in the measurements of the room they are working in and answer a few questions and this application will automatically calculate how much equipment they will need. It will also automatically calculate how much the job will cost and put it into a format that can be shown to a customer.

I am proud to say I have done multiple presentations on this application all over Florida and trained multiple technicians how to use it.
